# Participate as a University

Universities across the Midwest are invited to participate in the **Midwest Bioinformatics Showcase**.

Institutions can:

- Nominate students or postdocs to present in upcoming sessions.
- Share seminar announcements across their departments.
- Co-host events or provide moderators for future sessions.
- Collaborate on training or resource-sharing opportunities related to HPC and genomics.
